KYRA SAVOLAINEN

Expertise:

Kyra Savolainen is an undergraduate student of Urban and Regional Planning in Ryerson’s Faculty of Community Services. Outside of class, Kyra is employed by Library’s Digital Media Experience Lab as Student Advisor and Social Media Coordinator, and has been elected by the Ryerson Association of Planning Students to serve as Communications Co-Chair for 2015-2016.

Prior to pursuing her degree, Savolainen earned an advanced diploma in Advertising at Centennial College and worked in Toronto’s vibrant digital marketing industry where she specialized in social media management.

Asked to briefly describe her interest in digital media, Kyra explains that she “would love to help communities achieve greater technological literacy and access with the aim of creating sustainable balances in health, prosperity, and governance.”
