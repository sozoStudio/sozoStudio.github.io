About DME

Digital Media Experience Lab

The Digital Media Experience Lab (DME) is located on the 3rd floor of the Student Learning Centre (SLC).

This Lab is a Library resource that aims to:


  • support curricula and extracurricular student learning through workshops, peer tutoring and one-on-one instruction.
  • help Ryerson students learn basic and advanced digital skill-sets while exposing them to new and emerging technologies
  • make digital media tools available to all Ryerson students

This space provides bookable high-end multimedia workstations, peer-mentoring and support, workshops, specialized equipment such as 3D printers and scanners, and borrowable tech (cameras, MaKey MaKeys, arduinos, Raspberry Pis).

DME Subjects as Characters

The DME characters are avatars each cooresponding to the technology been practiced and taught at DME. They are designed and created for the purpose of engaging broader audiences. As the leading technological hub of Ryerson library. DME would like to welcome students not only from engineering or science background, but also from more tranditional faculties.
